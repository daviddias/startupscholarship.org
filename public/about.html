The Team

The Startup Scholarship was born from the wish of some of the best and most engaged students in engineering and management in Portugal to build a new approach for Startups to find talent while at the same time providing an innovative experience and gateway into entrepreneurship for top University students around the world. A summer of intense work, learning and networking, powered by the hard work of a world-class team.
